About This Project

An innovative solution combining artificial intelligence and meteorological data to predict avalanche occurrences in India's mountainous regions.

The Challenge

Avalanches pose a significant threat to life and infrastructure in the Himalayan regions of India. Traditional methods of risk assessment are often reactive and lack precision. Our system addresses this critical gap by leveraging machine learning algorithms to analyze terrain characteristics and provide predictive insights.

Our Solution

Using the Random Forest algorithm, our system analyzes multiple terrain parameters including elevation, slope, aspect, and location-specific data to generate accurate risk assessments for regions like Leh, Gulmarg, Manali, and Shimla.
